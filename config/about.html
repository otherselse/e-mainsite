About Lgsteel
Shanghai LG Steel is a leader of manufacturing and selling in the field of metal material which was established in 1991. We are specializing in stainless steel, carbon steel, alloy steel, special steel and other steel products and metal raw materials. The products types include steelplate, sheet, coil, galvanized coil, rebar(straight and in coil), round steel, profile steel, steel pipes, pipe fittings, flanges, bolts and nus(fastener), pipe pile, sheet pile, API oil pipeline, petroleum casing, petroleum drill pipe, petroleum drill bit, petroleum drill collar, mechanical processing piece, turning piece, cutting piece, Victaulic groove pipes, Victaulic couplings.
